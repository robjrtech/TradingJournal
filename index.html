symbol → also accepts: contractname, contract, ticker
side → long/short/buy/sell  |  also accepts: type, direction
qty → also accepts: size, quantity, contracts
entry / exit → also accepts: entryprice / exitprice Example row: 2026-04-01, AAPL, long, 100, 182.40, 185.20, 280.00
