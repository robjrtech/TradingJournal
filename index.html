📥 Import Trades from CSV

Upload a CSV file to populate your trading journal.

Required CSV columns (header row mandatory, order flexible): date, symbol, side, qty, entry, exit, pnl date → YYYY-MM-DD, MM/DD/YYYY, or MM/DD/YYYY HH:MM:SS ±TZ  |  also accepts: tradeday, trade_date
symbol → also accepts: contractname, contract, ticker
side → long/short/buy/sell  |  also accepts: type, direction
qty → also accepts: size, quantity, contracts
entry / exit → also accepts: entryprice / exitprice Example row: 2026-04-01, AAPL, long, 100, 182.40, 185.20, 280.00
